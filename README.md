
## Baon-Venture

## About
Baon-Venture is an interactive educational game designed for Grade 4 learners. The game places players in the shoes of a young learner navigating daily situations and challenges of managing a school allowance (baon).

By combining Financial Literacy with GMRC (Good Manners and Right Conduct), the game teaches children that budgeting isn't just about numbers, but about the Superpower of Choice. Players must balance their personal wants, school needs, and opportunities to help others, ultimately discovering what it truly means to be a responsible and mindful Budget Hero.
